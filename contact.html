Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ParqPilot

How does ParqPilot work?

ParqPilot uses advanced technology to help you find, reserve, and pay for parking spots. Simply open the app, see available spots in real-time, reserve your preferred location, and pay securely through the app.

Is ParqPilot available in my city?

ParqPilot is currently available in major cities across the United States. We're constantly expanding our coverage. Check the app store or contact us to see if we're available in your area.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets including Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

Can I cancel my parking reservation?

Yes, you can cancel your reservation up to 30 minutes before your scheduled parking time. Cancellations made within 30 minutes may be subject to a small cancellation fee.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. If you believe you're entitled to a refund, please contact our support team with your reservation details and reason for the refund request.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
