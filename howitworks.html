How ParqPilot Works

1. Launch the App

Open ParqPilot to see the splash screen and get ready to find your parking spot effortlessly.

2. Get Started

Tap Get Started to begin. If you’re a new user, sign up for a new account. If you already have an account, log in with your credentials.

3. Login or Sign Up

Enter your username and password to log in, or sign up with your email and create a password if you’re new.

4. Find a Parking Slot

Use the map to search for available parking slots near your destination. Tap on a location pin to view details.

5. Choose Your Slot

View available slots, select your preferred one, and tap Choose Slot to reserve it.

6. Manage Your Account

Access your profile, change your password, contact support, or learn more about ParqPilot from the My Account section.

7. View Activity

Check your parking history and activity to keep track of your reservations and parking locations.

8. Change Password

For security, you can easily change your password at any time from the Change Password screen.
